May Project Placeholder: check back May 28

My May Project idea is a bucket list of activities that pushes the limits of my normal life, particularly pertaining to the idea of outdoor/survivalist experiences. My list of activities include some things that I’ve tried before (such as cooking), but the idea is to challenge what I’ve grown accustomed to. Other activities, as previously mentioned, have more to do with survival such as fishing, horseback riding/horse care, CPR/first aid, archery, butchery, car maintenance, etc. This May Project idea interests me because I believe experience is invaluable, and simply due to the fast paced nature of life, the activities I’ve listed are experiences I doubt I’ll have a lot of access to as I get older and have less time. Some other tasks include mental overcoming, such as playing guitar in front of other people. I really want to learn to become more independent on a primordial basis.

Most of my activities are limited because I have soccer practice everyday throughout the school week from around 3-6 with the addition of other games

Take a look and enjoy!
